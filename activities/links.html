  1. Absolute links are used for links outside of our own site and are not used in the <nav>. Relative links are links that are used to link back to your own pages on your own site. These can be used in the <nav> tag.
  2. Relative links have 3 rules
    1. one . is used to link to something in the current folder
    2. two . .is used to link to something in the parent folder
    3. /is used to start at the root which we wont generally be doing in this class.
  3. Links don't always need to be inside the <nav> tag.
