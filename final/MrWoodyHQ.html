Why MrWoody?

The name MrWoody for my channel came from many different things all bundled up into one.
The Woody part came from two different things, my name and my dog. At this point is it no secret that my name has Wood in it somewhere. What you may not know is the "Woody" also comes from a dog I had years ago. His name was... Woody. For a few years now my channel has been known as MrWoody.
